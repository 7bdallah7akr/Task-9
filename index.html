Welcome

Palestine, also known as "Palestine", is a historical and geographical region located in the Middle East. Palestine has a rich and complex history, as this land has witnessed many
events and changes throughout the ages. Palestine is a sacred place for many world religions, including Islam, Christianity, and Judaism, making it an important place for many
people around the world.


The most famous historical sites in Palestine

Alquds
Alquds: Alquds is the Palestinian capital and the location of many important historical monuments including the Al-Aqsa Mosque, the Holy Shrine Church, and the Buraq Wall.
Alquds is considered sacred in three religions: Islam, Christianity, and Judaism

Bethlehem
Bethlehem: Bethlehem is considered the alleged birth city of Jesus Christ, and is famous for the Church of the Nativity and the Church of St. Catherine

Gaza
Gaza is a city located in the south of the Palestinian Gaza Strip on the eastern coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is one of the major cities in Palestine and has a long and diverse history. Ancient and modern.
